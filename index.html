About Farming Simulator 22

Farming Simulator 22 is a farming simulation video game series developed by Giants Software. It allows players to manage their own farm, grow crops, raise animals, and expand their farming business.

Game Features

  • Wide variety of crops to grow
  • Realistic farming equipment
  • Animal husbandry
  • Multiplayer mode
  • Seasonal cycles
